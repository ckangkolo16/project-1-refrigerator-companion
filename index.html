Recipe Area

  1. Step 1 Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

  2. Step 2 Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic until tender, then stir in chicken. Season with red pepper flakes. Cook until chicken is golden, and cooked through.

  3. Step 3 In a large bowl, combine pasta, chicken, sun-dried tomatoes and pesto. Toss to coat evenly.
